Deep Eye Research Program 2026

For outstanding undergraduate and master students interested in the 2027 Shenzhen Loop Area Institute joint-training PhD pathway.

  • Four-week research project with FreedomAI and Shenzhen Loop Area Institute mentorship.
  • Excellent performance can help lock in early admission opportunities for the Shenzhen Loop Area Institute program.
  • Free accommodation during the program, campus meal vouchers, and transportation subsidy for students from outside Shenzhen.
  • Interview-based recommendation support is available for suitable applicants.
Program dates July 13, 2026 to August 9, 2026; extendable to August 16, 2026.
Application deadline June 25, 2026.
Consultation wangbenyou@cuhk.edu.cn or externalaffairs@slai.edu.cn.

Why FreedomAI

Strong research resources

  • CUHK-Shenzhen PhD degree with global recognition.
  • Shenzhen Loop Area Institute joint-training resources, strong GPU support, and generous student support.
  • Project leads at Shenzhen Loop Area Institute with access to substantial research and engineering resources.

Industry and deployment access

  • Close collaboration with Huawei, Qwen, Tencent, MiniMax, Kimi, Ant Group, Ubiquant, and other partners.
  • Encouragement for senior PhD internships, startup exploration, and deploying AI into real-world systems.
  • Medical AI systems connected to hospitals, community healthcare, multilingual medicine, and clinical evaluation.

Active team culture

  • Friendly, high-energy research atmosphere with mentorship from senior students to newcomers.
  • Members grow through real projects, open-source releases, and paper ownership.
  • Sports, camps, night talks, and a strong belief that top research needs passion for both life and freedom.

What You Can Build Here

Open AI systems

FreedomIntelligence projects have received more than ten thousand GitHub stars, with Hugging Face model and dataset downloads reaching million-scale community usage.

Medical AI with real users

HuatuoGPT has been connected to 11 public hospitals and 186 community healthcare centers in Longgang, Shenzhen, serving a population-scale medical AI setting.

Frontier research programs

Representative directions include HuatuoGPT, CMB and LiveClin, Apollo multilingual medical AI, AceGPT, ORLM, AIMO-style reasoning, TwinMarket, and Economic World Models.
