Deep Eye Research Program 2026深瞳科研项目 2026

For outstanding undergraduate and master students interested in the 2027 Shenzhen Loop Area Institute joint-training PhD pathway.面向有意申请 2027 年深圳河套学院联培博士项目的优秀本科生与硕士生。

  • Four-week research project with FreedomAI and Shenzhen Loop Area Institute mentorship.为期四周的科研项目，由 FreedomAI 和深圳河套学院联合指导。
  • Designed for outstanding undergraduate and master students who want early, resource-rich exposure to frontier AI research.专为希望提前接触资源丰富的前沿 AI 科研环境的优秀本科生和硕士生设计。
  • Excellent performance can help lock in early admission opportunities for the Shenzhen Loop Area Institute program.表现优秀者可获得深圳河套学院项目提前录取机会。
  • Project leads at Shenzhen Loop Area Institute provide substantial research resources, engineering support, and long-term mentorship.深圳河套学院项目负责人提供充足科研资源、工程支持和长期指导。
  • Free accommodation during the program, campus meal vouchers, and transportation subsidy for students from outside Shenzhen.项目期间提供免费住宿、校园餐补，以及外地学生的交通补贴。
  • Interview-based recommendation support is available for suitable applicants.对合适的申请者提供面试推荐支持。
Program dates项目时间 July 13, 2026 to August 9, 2026; extendable to August 16, 2026.2026 年 7 月 13 日至 8 月 9 日，可延长至 8 月 16 日。
Application deadline申请截止日期 June 25, 2026.2026 年 6 月 25 日。
