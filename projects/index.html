Projects

FreedomAI projects are organized as research programs rather than isolated releases. Each program below bundles together the papers, GitHub repositories, Hugging Face models or datasets, benchmarks, and deployment artifacts that make the direction reproducible. The recurring story is simple: build open data, train domain models, evaluate them in realistic tasks, and then connect them to agents, simulators, and deployed workflows.

01 data Reusable substrates

Huatuo-26M, RAG-Instruct, ApolloCorpus, ShareGPT-4o-Image, TalkVid, and benchmark datasets make later model releases reproducible.

02 models Domain foundation models

HuatuoGPT, Apollo, AceGPT, LongLLaVA, Janus-4o, and Soundwave specialize open models for medicine, languages, images, and speech.

03 evaluation Pressure tests

CMB, MileBench, GMAI-MMBench, S2S-Arena, EchoMind, and reasoning benchmarks expose whether models actually solve domain tasks.

04 worlds Agents and simulations

TwinMarket, MicroVerse, and Economic World Models ask how models behave inside evolving social, financial, medical, and scientific worlds.
