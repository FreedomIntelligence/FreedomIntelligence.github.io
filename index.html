Welcome to Freedom AI. We are a group of researchers working on natural language processing, large language models, multimodal AI, and agents at the School of Data Science, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, SRIBD, and Shenzhen Loop Area Institute.

Freedom AI aims to free humanity from the boundaries of life through AI. Our current focus includes:

  • Democratizing large language models and open-source AI infrastructure.
  • Advancing medical, mathematical, reasoning, multilingual, and multimodal capabilities of LLMs.
  • Building agents, tool-use systems, HCI workflows, MCP applications, and RAG systems.
  • Evaluating and deepening the understanding of LLMs.
  • Deploying NLP, speech, and multimodal AI in real-world medical, educational, and social applications.

Highlights

  • Open source impact: FreedomIntelligence has accumulated 10K+ GitHub stars; Hugging Face downloads have reached the million scale, with monthly model downloads exceeding 200K.
  • Real-world deployment: HuatuoGPT-powered intelligent triage and pre-consultation systems have been deployed in 11 public hospitals and 186 community health centers in Longgang, Shenzhen, serving a population of about 5 million.
  • Global medical AI: Apollo expands multilingual medical LLMs to 50 languages, moving medical AI toward broader global access.
  • Awards: SIGIR 2017 Best Paper Honorable Mention, NAACL 2019 Best Explainable NLP Paper, NLPCC 2022 Best Paper, ICLR 2025 Financial AI Workshop Best Paper, and NeurIPS 2025 ResponsibleFM Workshop Outstanding Paper.
  • Entrepreneurship: We encourage members to build products that serve society. See Startups.
  • Culture: Work hard and play harder. See Life.
